Our Story

Perfume the Old Way

Oud House was born from a love of traditional attar-making. Every bottle is blended by hand in Dublin — concentrated perfume oils in the Middle Eastern tradition, free of alcohol and free of shortcuts.

  • 100% oil-based, alcohol-free concentrated perfumes
  • Blended and bottled by hand in small batches
  • Long-lasting sillage — a little goes a long way
  • Classic oud, amber, musk & rose accords, plus modern interpretations
